# McGrathNicol/YouGov Supply Chain Study (August 2024)

**Source:** McGrathNicol/YouGov study, August 2024  
**Reference:** Third-party requirements reshaping Australian organisations

## Key Finding

> 82% of Australian companies do not extend risk assessments beyond Tier 1 suppliers

This is the most cited statistic on Australian fourth-party (Nth-party) risk blindness. It means the vast majority of Australian organisations have no visibility into their sub-processors and subcontractors.

## What This Means

- Fourth-party risk is largely unmanaged across the Australian market
- [[apra-cps-230]] and [[soci-act]] both require consideration of sub-processor risks
- TPRM vendors have a significant market opportunity to address this gap
- [[vendor-tiering]] practices are inadequate at most organisations

## Context

The study was conducted August 2024, just over a year before the [[apra-cps-230]] 1 July 2025 effective date, suggesting significant last-minute compliance scrambling.
