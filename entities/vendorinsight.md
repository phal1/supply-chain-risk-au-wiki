# VendorInsight

## Overview

VendorInsight is an Australian web-based vendor risk management solution. Provides automated workflows for vendor assessment, contract management, and ongoing monitoring.

## Products

- Web-based vendor and contract management
- Automated assessment workflows
- Risk scoring and tiering
- Compliance tracking

## Market Position

- Australian specialist
- Targets mid-market and SMEs
- Competitor to TPRM360 in local market
