# Interos

## Overview

Interos is an AI-powered supply chain resilience platform with TPRM capabilities. Uses machine learning to map and monitor supply chains at scale across all tiers. TPRA member.

## Products

### Third-Party Risk Management
- AI-driven supplier risk assessment
- Multi-tier supply chain mapping (beyond Tier 1)
- Geopolitical and concentration risk analysis
- Financial health monitoring
- Operational resilience scoring

### Partnership with ProcessUnity
Interos and ProcessUnity have partnered to provide data-driven TPRM:
- Interos supply chain intelligence feeds into ProcessUnity TPRM workflows
- Combined fourth-party mapping + assessment workflow

## Market Position

- Differentiated by: AI and multi-tier supply chain mapping
- Competes with Black Kite and BitSight on supply chain visibility
- Growing ANZ presence

## Australian Relevance

- Multi-tier mapping directly addresses the 82% gap (McGrathNicol finding)
- Geopolitical risk features relevant for supply chains with foreign involvement (SOCI Act focus)
- [[soci-act]] supply chain requirements align with Interos capabilities
