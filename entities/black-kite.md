# Black Kite

## Overview

Black Kite is a global third-party cyber risk management platform specialising in security ratings and TPRM. Differentiator: strong focus on product-level and software supply chain risk analysis. TPRA member.

## Products

### Cyber Risk Ratings
- Algorithmically-derived security ratings updated continuously
- Focus on external observable signals
- Third-party and fourth-party monitoring

### TPRM Platform
- Vendor risk assessments and monitoring
- Fourth-party/sub-processor mapping (identifies when vendors use same sub-processors)
- Compliance reporting (APRA, SOCI, GDPR, etc.)

### Product Analysis Module (recent addition)
- Assesses software risk at the product level
- Maps software bill of materials (SBOM) for third-party software
- Identifies vulnerabilities in third-party software components

## Market Position

- Competes directly with BitSight and SecurityScorecard in security ratings
- Differentiated by: product-level risk analysis, fourth-party mapping
- Growing ANZ presence

## Australian Relevance

- Pre-built APRA and SOCI compliance reporting
- Fourth-party mapping directly addresses the 82% gap (McGrathNicol)
- Supply chain cyber risk analysis fits [[soci-act]] requirements
