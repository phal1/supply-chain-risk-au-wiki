# Cliffside Cybersecurity

## Overview

Cliffside Cybersecurity is an Australian advisory firm specialising in third-party security risk. Provides TPRM guidance specific to the Australian regulatory landscape.

## Services

- Third-party security risk assessments
- TPRM program design
- Vendor attack surface analysis
- Guidance on Australian regulations ([[apra-cps-230]], [[soci-act]])

## Position

- Australian boutique focused on third-party security risk
- Publishes TPRM guidance specific to Australian requirements
- Competitor to Kroll and larger consulting firms on targeted third-party risk work
