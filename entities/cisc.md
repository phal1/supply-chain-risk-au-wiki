# CISC — Critical Infrastructure Commission

## Overview

The Critical Infrastructure Commission (CISC) is the Commonwealth regulator for critical infrastructure under the [[soci-act]]. It replaced the former role of the Secretary of the Department of Home Affairs for SOCI purposes. CISC is responsible for administering the Register of Critical Infrastructure Assets and overseeing compliance with positive security obligations.

## Role

CISC's role in TPRM context:
- Maintains the **Register of Critical Infrastructure Assets** — entities must report ownership and operational information
- Administers the **risk management program** obligations under Part 2 of the SOCI Act
- Coordinates with the **Australian Cyber Security Centre (ACSC)** for cyber incident reporting
- Oversees compliance with the **Enhanced Cyber Security Obligations** for Systems of National Significance

## Supply Chain Responsibilities

CISC specifically addresses supply chain as one of four core hazard domains (alongside cyber/IT, personnel, and physical). Entities must include supply chain risk in their written risk management programs.
