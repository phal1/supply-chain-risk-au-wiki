# McGrathNicol — TPRM Advisory

## Overview

McGrathNicol is an Australian advisory firm specialising in restructuring, risk, and digital. Published the widely-cited McGrathNicol/YouGov study (August 2024) showing 82% of Australian companies don't extend risk assessments beyond Tier 1 suppliers.

## Notable Research

### McGrathNicol/YouGov Supply Chain Study (August 2024)
- **Key finding:** 82% of Australian companies do not extend risk assessments beyond Tier 1 suppliers
- This is the most cited statistic on Australian fourth-party risk blindness
- Study conducted with YouGov, August 2024 — just over a year before CPS 230 effective date
