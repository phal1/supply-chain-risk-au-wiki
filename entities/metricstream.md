# MetricStream

## Overview

MetricStream is a global GRC platform with TPRM capabilities. Targets large enterprises and is used in ANZ financial services and government.

## Products

### GRC Platform
- Enterprise risk management
- Policy management
- Audit management
- Compliance management
- **TPRM / Vendor risk management module**

## ANZ Position

- ANZ team and customer base
- Competitor to ServiceNow, RSA Archer, Diligent in GRC
- Used by large enterprises already in the MetricStream ecosystem
