# UpGuard

## Overview

UpGuard is a global third-party risk management and cyber resilience platform. **Founded in Australia** (Brisbane), now headquartered in the US. One of the few globally-known TPRM vendors with Australian origins.

## Products

### Core TPRM Platform
- Vendor risk assessments (questionnaire-based and document upload)
- Attack surface monitoring (external asset discovery)
- Security ratings (algorithmically derived from external signals)
- Data leak detection (searches for exposed credentials/documents)
- Compliance mapping (to various frameworks)

### Differentiators
- Australian-founded, strong ANZ customer base
- Breach detection capabilities — can identify when vendor data appears in leak forums
- Integration with供应链 threat intelligence

## Market Position

- Competes with BitSight, SecurityScorecard in the security ratings space
- Positions as "holistic TPCRM" (Third Party Cyber Risk Management)
- Used by large enterprises and government agencies globally

## Australian Relevance

- Australian government agencies use UpGuard for vendor risk management
- Strong fit with [[cisc]] SOCI Act requirements for supply chain risk management
- Useful for demonstrating due diligence for [[apra-cps-230]] compliance
