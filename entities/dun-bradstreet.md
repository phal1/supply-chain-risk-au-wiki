# Dun & Bradstreet

## Overview

Dun & Bradstreet (D&B) is a global data and analytics company with significant supply chain intelligence and risk data relevant to TPRM. Provides financial health data, supplier information, and risk scores.

## Products

### Supply Chain Risk
- Financial health monitoring of suppliers
- Supplier risk scores and credit ratings
- Supply chain mapping and concentration analysis
- Global supplier database

### D&B TPRM Integration
- Financial risk data feeds into TPRM platforms
- Used for initial vendor due diligence and ongoing monitoring
- Complements cyber risk ratings from BitSight/Black Kite

## Market Position

- Dominant in corporate credit and supplier data
- Strong ANZ presence (long-established in Australia)
- Often used alongside cyber ratings vendors

## Australian Relevance

- [[apra-cps-230]] concentration risk: D&B data helps identify financial health of critical service providers
- [[soci-act]] supply chain: foreign involvement and ownership data
- Fourth-party concentration: D&B supply chain maps can identify shared sub-processors
