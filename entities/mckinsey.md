# McKinsey & Company — ANZ

## Overview

McKinsey & Company is a global strategy and management consulting firm. Has an APAC hub in Sydney. While not a TPRM specialist, advises boards and senior leadership on operational risk, supply chain resilience, and digital transformation — all relevant to TPRM strategy.

## Relevance to TPRM

- Board-level TPRM strategy
- Operating model design for risk functions
- Supply chain resilience and stress testing
- Technology selection for TPRM programs
- Often engaged for initial TPRM program design before handing off to implementation partners
