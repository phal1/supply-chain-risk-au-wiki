# OneTrust

## Overview

OneTrust is a global privacy, security, and governance platform with TPRM capabilities. Australian presence: Melbourne office, two local data centres, ANZ-specific product alignment.

## Products

### Privacy Management
- Consent management
- Data mapping and inventory
- Breach response and notification
- Privacy impact assessments

### Third-Party Management (TPRM)
- Vendor risk assessments
- Privacy-focused TPRM (data handling due diligence)
- Fourth-party/sub-processor visibility
- Integration between privacy and TPRM workflows

### CPS 230 Content
OneTrust has published on [[apra-cps-230]]:
- "Navigating Australia's CPS 230 to Combat Third-Party Risk"
- TPRM + privacy compliance alignment
- Vendor audit workflows for CPS 230

## Market Position

- Primary strength: privacy + TPRM combined
- Competes with NAVEX, Riskonnect where privacy is primary driver
- Strong in enterprises where Privacy/Legal owns TPRM (vs Information Security)

## Australian Relevance

- Local data centres address financial services data residency requirements
- Privacy + TPRM combination directly relevant to [[oaic-notifiable-data-breaches]]
- [[apra-cps-230]] content specifically produced for Australian market
