# BitSight

## Overview

BitSight is a global leader in security ratings and third-party risk management. The company uses algorithmically-derived security ratings based on external observable signals (e.g., compromised systems, open ports, vulnerability disclosures) to rate organisations' cybersecurity performance.

## Products

### Security Ratings
- Continuous, algorithmically-computed ratings for any organisation
- Updates daily as new signals are collected
- Used for benchmarking, peer comparison, and vendor monitoring

### TPRM Workflow
- Vendor monitoring with automated alerts on rating changes
- Questionnaire automation
- Remediation tracking
- Executive reporting

### BitSight for TPRM
- Focus on continuous monitoring vs point-in-time assessments
- Integrates with GRC platforms (ServiceNow, RSA Archer)
- Fourth-party (subscriber) discovery — identifies when vendors' clients overlap

## Market Position

- Largest security ratings vendor by revenue and market presence
- Primary competitor: SecurityScorecard
- Differentiator: longest track record, largest panel of observable data

## Australian Relevance

- Widely adopted by large Australian financial institutions
- ANZ team and local support
- Used for [[apra-cps-230]] compliance evidence and [[oaic]] data breach risk assessment
