# ProcessUnity

## Overview

ProcessUnity is a dedicated TPRM platform focused on vendor lifecycle management, risk assessments, and regulatory alignment. Used by large enterprises globally including Australian insurers.

## Products

### Third-Party Risk Management Platform
- Vendor intake and classification
- Risk questionnaires and assessments
- Continuous monitoring integration (via BitSight, SecurityScorecard, etc.)
- Fourth-party/sub-processor risk
- Contract management integration
- Regulatory reporting (APRA, SOC 2, ISO 27001)

### Global Risk Exchange
- Pre-built assessment templates
- Automated scoring and tiering
- Workflow orchestration

## ANZ Presence

Notable case study: **QBE Insurance** (one of Australia's largest insurers) uses ProcessUnity's Global Risk Exchange to:
- Improve oversight of third parties
- Scale vendor risk assessments
- Boost efficiency of third-party oversight

Published APRA-related content on CPS 234 and CPS 230 alignment.
