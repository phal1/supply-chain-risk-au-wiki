# TPRM360

## Overview

TPRM360 is an **Australian-focused TPRM platform** founded to serve the local market. Positioned as the "single pane of glass" for Australian businesses managing vendor risk, with APRA and SOCI compliance built in natively.

## Products

### TPRM Platform
- Unified vendor risk dashboard
- Contract management and compliance document repository
- Automated monitoring: cyber risks, financial health, regulatory changes
- **Fourth-party/sub-processor mapping** — specifically addresses the 82% gap
- [[apra-cps-230]] compliance templates and audit-ready reporting
- [[soci-act]] alignment and reporting
- [[oaic-notifiable-data-breaches]] workflows

### TPRM Diagnostic
- 30-day assessment: people, processes, technology vs global benchmarks
- Risk exposure report with prioritised vendor vulnerabilities
- Compliance health check against APRA CPS 230, SOCI, privacy laws
- Roadmap to TPRM maturity

### TPRM Consulting
- Policy and workflow design
- Vendor tiering and onboarding frameworks
- Contract clause library (cyber, continuity, data privacy)
- Training and change management

## Market Position

- **Differentiated as Australian-local**: built for local regulatory landscape, not ported from US/EU
- Competitors in ANZ: UpGuard, Riskonnect, ProcessUnity, Black Kite
- Unique position: Australian-specific compliance templates for APRA and SOCI
