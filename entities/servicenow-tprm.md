# ServiceNow TPRM

## Overview

ServiceNow's Third-Party Risk Management (TPRM) application replaces the former Vendor Risk Management (VRM) module. Strong enterprise play — leverages existing ServiceNow customer relationships.

## Products

### ServiceNow TPRM Application
- Centralised third-party portfolio management
- Risk assessments and scoring
- Continuous monitoring integration
- Fourth-party/sub-processor management
- Regulatory alignment reporting
- Workflow automation

### Digital Resilience Third-Party Information Register
- New application within Vendor Management Workspace
- Tracks assessments and third-party information
- Supports [[apra-cps-230]] third-party register requirements

## Market Position

- Dominant in enterprises already using ServiceNow ITSM/GRC
- Competitive against dedicated TPRM platforms where ITAM is important
- ANZ: strong installed base in financial services and government

## Competitive Advantage

- Already embedded in enterprise workflows
- Single platform for IT ops + risk + TPRM
- [[apra-cps-230]] alignment content available
- AC3 (NSW government ICT provider) has published on ServiceNow TPRM

## Limitations

- TPRM is a module, not a dedicated TPRM platform — may lack depth vs ProcessUnity/Riskonnect
- Requires existing ServiceNow footprint
