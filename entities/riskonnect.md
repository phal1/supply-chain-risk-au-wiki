# Riskonnect

## Overview

Riskonnect is a global TPRM and integrated risk management platform. Positions as a "connected risks" platform linking TPRM with ESG, business continuity, and enterprise risk. Strong presence in ANZ financial services.

## Products

### Third-Party Risk Management
- Vendor intake and onboarding workflows
- Risk assessments and tiering
- Ongoing monitoring and alerting
- Fourth-party/sub-processor visibility
- Configurable dashboards and reporting

### Integrated Risk Connect
- TPRM + ESG + Business Continuity on single platform
- Regulatory alignment engine (APRA, SOCI, GDPR)
- Audit-ready reporting
- Scenario analysis

## Market Position

- Strong in ANZ financial services and insurance
- Positions as alternative to point-solution TPRM tools
- Competitors: ProcessUnity, ServiceNow TPRM, NAVEX

## Australian Relevance

Riskonnect has published extensively on [[apra-cps-230]] alignment:
- APRA compliance content on their website
- TPRM alignment with operational risk management requirements
- Configurable for [[oaic-notifiable-data-breaches]] workflows
- Pre-built templates for APRA-regulated entities
