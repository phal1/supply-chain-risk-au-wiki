# Australian Regulatory Map — Supply Chain & TPRM

## Primary Regulations

### APRA CPS 230 — Operational Risk Management
- **Effective:** 1 July 2025
- **Regulator:** APRA
- **Applies to:** ADIs, insurers, superannuation funds (~640 entities)
- **Key focus:** Service provider management, business continuity, critical operations
- **Supply chain relevance:** Mandates TPRM programs for financial sector

### SOCI Act — Security of Critical Infrastructure Act 2018
- **Effective:** Expanded 2022-2023 amendments, ongoing rollout
- **Regulator:** CISC
- **Applies to:** 11 critical infrastructure sectors
- **Key focus:** Physical, personnel, supply chain, cyber/information security
- **Supply chain relevance:** Explicitly requires supply chain risk management programs

### Privacy Act 1988 (Cth)
- **Effective:** Ongoing (APP entities)
- **Regulator:** OAIC
- **Applies to:** APP entities (organisations with >$3M turnover, health providers, etc.)
- **Key focus:** Personal information handling, data breach notification
- **Supply chain relevance:** Entities liable for breaches through third-party processors

### Cyber Security Act 2024
- **Effective:** Passed November 2024
- **Regulator:** Home Affairs / ASD
- **Key focus:** Extends cyber security rules to third-party vendor risk management

## Secondary / Supporting

### APRA CPS 234 — Information Security
- Complements CPS 230 on IT security requirements
- Requires security controls for information assets

### ASIC Report 776 — Spotlight on Cyber
- Non-mandatory but sets expectations for cyber risk management
- Requires third-party risk assessments for market intermediaries

### IIA Topical Requirements (Third Party)
- **Effective:** September 2026
- **Applies to:** Internal audit functions
- Mandates third-party management audit as mandatory IPPF element

## Regulatory Timeline

```
2023-24: SOCI Act expanded, CISC compliance education phase
2024-11: Cyber Security Act 2024 passed
2025-07: APRA CPS 230 effective
2025-09: IIA Topical Requirements issued
2026-07: APRA CPS 230 pre-existing contracts deadline
2026-09: IIA Topical Requirements effective
Ongoing: SOCI Act enforcement (education + enforcement balanced)
```
